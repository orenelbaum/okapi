# Current Goal

- Tokens: identifiers, integers, operators, keywords

- Operators: `=`, `+`, `-`, `/`, `*`

- Keywords: `if`

- Variable declarations

- Arithmetic

- If statements

- Formatting:
	x =
		1 + 2

	x =
		1 + 2
		- 3 + 4
	//	↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓
	const x = 1 + 2 - 3 + 4

	x =
		1 + 2
		* 3 + 4
	//	↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓ ↓
	const x = (1 + 2) * (3 + 4)
